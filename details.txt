ConvertThis is the first app from Dreamers Paradise Entertainment.

ConvertThis allows you to quickly convert between units in the following measurements:

Mass: Gram(g), Ounce(oz), Pound(lb), Kilogram(kg), Miligram(mg), Stone(st), US ton(t), Imperial ton(t), and Metric ton(mt).

Time: Second, Minute, Hour, Day, Week, Month, and Year.

Temperature: Celsius(°C), Fahrenheit(°F), and kelvin(K).

Length: Mile, Yard, Foot, Inch, Nautical Mile, Kilometer(km), Meter(m), Centimeter(cm), Millimeter(mm), Micrometer(μm), and Nanometer(nm).

Volume: barrel(bbl), gallon(gal), quart(qt), pint(pt), cup, gill(gi), tablespoon(tbsp), teaspoon(tsp), cubic meter, cubic foot, cubic yard, liter(L), milliliter(mL), and fluid ounce(fl oz).

Speed: Kilometer per hour, Miles per hour(mph), Foot per second, Meter per second, and Knot.

Data Storage: bit(b), byte(B), kilobyte(kB), megabyte(MB), gigabyte(GB), terabyte(TB), and petabyte(PB).
